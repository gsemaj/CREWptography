ඞ CREWptography ඞ







Ever been worried about an impostor snooping on your comms? Fear no more, as with CREWptography you and another crewmate are able to securely encrypt your messages and fix the comms sabotage.

To Use: Press the "Generate a Crewmate" Button, and copy the crewmate who appears. Send it to someone else, and have them enter it in the "Other Crewmate" Field. After pressing the "This is the other Crewmate" button, you're now able to send encrypted messages back and forth using the Emergency message fields!

Created using JavaScript, HTML, and the Sodium library, for HackOHI/O 2021.
